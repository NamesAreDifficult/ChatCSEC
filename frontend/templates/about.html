About ChatCSEC

This project is a conversational interface that allows users to interact with an AI model. Developed as part of the ChatCSEC initiative, it aims to provide users with a friendly and accessible way to engage with AI technology.

The project was authored by Domenic Lo Iacono, Kyri Lea, Brian McNulty, and Rich Kleinhenz. ChatCSEC is designed to expertly answer cybersecurity questions and assist with tasks.
